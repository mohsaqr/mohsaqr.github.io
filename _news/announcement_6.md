Our second open-access book is published by Springer! [Advanced Learning Analytics Methods: AI, Precision and Complexity](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-3-031-95365-1) contains 22 chapters across 593 pages covering explainable AI, large language models in education, complex dynamic systems, transition network analysis, and idiographic precision analytics. Like its predecessor, every chapter includes full R tutorials with reproducible code and real educational datasets. Edited with Sonsoles L&oacute;pez-Pernas and freely available under CC BY 4.0.
